Pulse Tasks

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026

Pulse Tasks respects your privacy. This app is designed to help you manage personal tasks with minimal data handling.

Information We Collect

Pulse Tasks stores the task information you create, including titles, notes, categories, priorities, due dates, reminder times, and completion status. In the current version of the app, this information is stored locally on your device.

Pulse Tasks does not require account creation.

Pulse Tasks does not include third-party advertising SDKs.

Pulse Tasks does not collect analytics or tracking data in the current version.

Notifications

If you choose to enable notifications, Pulse Tasks uses local notifications on your device to remind you about tasks you created. Notification content is generated from your own task data.

Data Sharing

Pulse Tasks does not sell your personal information.

Pulse Tasks does not share your task data with third parties in the current version.

Data Retention

Your task data remains on your device unless you delete the app or remove the tasks yourself.

Your Choices

You can disable notifications at any time in iPhone Settings.

You can delete tasks within the app at any time.

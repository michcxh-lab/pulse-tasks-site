Common Questions

Why am I not receiving reminders?

Open iPhone Settings, find Pulse Tasks, and make sure notifications are allowed. Also confirm that the reminder time is set in the future.

Where is my task data stored?

In the current version, your task data is stored locally on your device.

Can I delete my tasks?

Yes. You can delete individual tasks or clear completed tasks from within the app.
